A robot is a machine designed to perform tasks automatically or with human control. Robots can be used in many different fields, such as manufacturing, healthcare, education, space exploration, and household activities. They can perform repetitive and difficult tasks quickly and accurately. Modern robots are designed with sensors, cameras, and artificial intelligence to understand their surroundings and perform specific actions. Some robots can walk, talk, move objects, and even interact with humans. Robots help people save time, reduce effort, and improve productivity. As technology continues to develop, robots are becoming smarter and more useful in our daily lives.
